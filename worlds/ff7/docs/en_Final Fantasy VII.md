# Final Fantasy VII

Final Fantasy VII (1997) combines field exploration, scripted boss fights, and richly connected hubs. Archipelago
randomizes treasure chests, key items, and shop inventories while retaining a straightforward completion goal: defeat
Sephiroth in the Northern Crater.

## Supported Features

- **Field randomization** toggles the core pool of field pickups.
- **Key item randomization** allows important story items to appear anywhere in the multiworld.
- **Shop shuffle** mixes inventories for Midgar, Kalm, Gold Saucer, and more.
- **Enemy stat variance** provides lightweight difficulty tuning.

## Victory Condition

Choose between defeating Sephiroth or collecting the Black Materia. The default goal locks the "Defeat Sephiroth"
location behind receiving the special Victory event item.

## Client Notes

The placeholder FF7 client simply connects to the server, accepts items, and can be extended later to interact with a
native Gold Saucer/FF7 integration.
